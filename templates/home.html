Down It!

Welcome to the Lucky Clover Inn. Do you have what it takes to make the leaderboard of boozy brainboxes?

Down It! is a multiple choice questions quiz on the subject of Ireland. For each correct answer, you take a gulp of the black stuff. Five gulps and you'll see away the pint. The pint glasses are stacked to see how many you can get through in 60 seconds! Just enter your name and hit "Mine's a pint!!"
